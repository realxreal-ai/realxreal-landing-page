Real people. Verified.

01

The world runs on lies.

The internet was built on trust. Not real trust — just the illusion of it.

Email assumes you are who you say you are. Phone numbers assume you belong to the device. Video calls assume the voice is real.

None of it was designed to be secure. We just never stopped to ask: Who’s actually on the other side?

02

Deepfakes didn’t break the system.

They exposed it.

The protocols holding up our digital world — TCP/IP, SMTP, DNS, SS7 — were built decades ago.

No encryption. No identity. No verification.

Now, AI doesn’t need to hack you. It just needs to sound like you.

One in ten people have already received an AI voice clone. 77% lost money because of it. — McAfee, 2024

03

People are the new attack surface.

Scammers don’t exploit firewalls anymore. They exploit emotion, trust, and panic.

45% of people say they’d respond to a fake call or message from a loved one asking for money.

Technology made us reachable. AI made us impersonable. And no one built a defense for human trust.

04

Enter realxreal.ai

A mobile app that lets you authenticate who’s really calling, emailing, or meeting you — before you ever respond.

We combine cryptography, biometrics, and a new class of human-centered verification called the Doppelganger Protocol.

No cloud uploads. No third-party servers. No “trust us.” Just you, your device, and the truth.

05

For individuals

Corporate-grade deepfake defense — for everyone.

You’ll never have to wonder if a call from your partner, friend, or child is real again.

06

For organizations

Deepfake-proof your people, clients, and supply chain.

Before you join a call, sign a contract, or wire a payment — realxreal confirms that both parties are authentic, verified, and device-bound.

Affordable, private, and secure by design. No enterprise budget required.

07

AI vs. AI is a losing game.

Most security companies are trying to fight deepfakes with more AI — detecting fakes after they happen, in the cloud, with huge compute costs.

We think that’s backwards.

You don’t stop a lie by chasing it. You stop it by knowing the truth before it speaks.

08

How realxreal works

Every new digital contact — friend, coworker, vendor — passes through your trust gateway.

  • Device-bound cryptographic identity
  • Biometric voice & face verification
  • Secure anomaly detection
  • Visual trust graph showing who’s real
  • Pre-call authentication requests

All running on-device — not the cloud.
