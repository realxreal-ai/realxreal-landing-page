The Identity Binding Problem

In a world of AI-driven deepfakes and sophisticated phishing, a core problem has emerged: when someone contacts you digitally, you can't be certain they are who they claim to be. This erodes trust and makes every interaction a potential risk.

The Solution: Quantifiable Trust

Our mobile app provides zero-knowledge, privacy-focused verification. Using a multi-layered approach, realxreal.ai allows you to authenticate contacts with measurable confidence *before* you communicate.

Cryptography & Biometrics

Device-bound passkeys, biometric voice prints, and typing fingerprints create a unique, secure digital identity.

Shared Memory Verification

Users answer randomized, personal questions only a true contact would know, with responses analyzed for semantic accuracy.

Authenticated Requests

Send or receive a verification request before a phone call or video chat to ensure you're connecting with the right person.

Trusted Network

Build a verified contact list, make trusted introductions, and even authenticate communications from companies and entities.
