Privacy Policy

At realxreal.ai, your privacy is a fundamental right — not a feature. This document outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and what we absolutely don’t do.

1. We Do Not Collect Personal Data by Default

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, trackers, or fingerprinting scripts. When you visit our website, we do not log your IP address, browser, or behavior. We respect your right to browse privately.

2. Early Access Signups

If you choose to sign up for early access, we collect only your email address. That’s it. We will use it solely to notify you when the product is ready or to ask for feedback, if you consent.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your information to any third party. Your data stays with us, encrypted and secured.

4. No Behavioral Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any behavioral tracking service. We measure success based on trust, not traffic data.

5. Email Retention and Deletion

You can request to delete your email address at any time by contacting founder@realxreal.ai. We'll honor your request within 72 hours.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we’ll update this page and notify all early access users by email — with opt-out options, always.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, email us at founder@realxreal.ai.
